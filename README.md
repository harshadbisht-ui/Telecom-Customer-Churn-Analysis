📊 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn in a telecom company to identify the major factors contributing to customer attrition and provide data-driven recommendations for improving customer retention.

The project demonstrates an end-to-end analytics workflow using Excel, MySQL, and Power BI.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze customer churn patterns.
Identify high-risk customer segments.
Build interactive dashboards for business users.
Provide actionable recommendations to improve retention.

Developed business recommendations based on analytical findings.

📈 Key Findings
47.44% of customers with 0–12 months tenure churned.
Month-to-month contracts recorded the highest churn rate (42.71%).
Fiber optic customers churned at 41.89%, more than twice the rate of DSL customers.
Customers without Tech Support churned nearly three times more than customers with Tech Support.
Electronic check users experienced the highest churn among all payment methods.
💡 Business Recommendations
Improve first-year customer onboarding and retention.
Encourage migration to long-term contracts.
Enhance the Fiber optic customer experience.
Promote Tech Support adoption.
Incentivize customers to switch to automatic payment methods.
